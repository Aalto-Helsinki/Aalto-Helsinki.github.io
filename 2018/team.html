The Aalto-Helsinki 2018 is a multidisciplinary team of 10 people with a shared ambition to conduct a unique and multidisciplinary research project. Our team is more multidisciplinary than ever before: It comprises of expertise from various fields including bioinformatics, molecular biosciences, genetics, biotechnology and chemical engineering, experimental physics, business, design and sustainability.
