Our Team

The Aalto-Helsinki 2017 is a multidisciplinary team of 9 people with a shared ambition to conduct a unique and multidisciplinary research project. Our team is more multidisciplinary than ever before: It comprises of expertise from various fields including biology, biotechnology, bioinformatics, mathematics, chemistry and programming coupled with experience in fashion, clothing and textile design, graphic design and mechanical engineering.

Meo Ekroos
Aalto University

Meo is a 3rd year Bachelor's student of BioIT in Aalto University, and has computer science as a minor. He is interested in medicine, nutrition, sports, meditation and well-being. In iGEM he is the main person responsible for the team web pages. He will probably play a part in the modelling business and do some lab work, research and writing, as well.

Finishing two other minors in Bioinformatics and Mycology. Interested in the environment, plants, fungi and insects. Currently pursuing her Master's in Molecular Biotechnology at University of Helsinki. Her Bachelor’s degree is in Genetics and Bioengineering. Oona Kinnunen

Meditation is the cornerstone of her life, and she is also interested in spiritual teachings. Her main responsibility in iGEM will be planning and performing experiments in the lab.
