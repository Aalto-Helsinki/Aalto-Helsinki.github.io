Support Us

We are an independent, student driven project of passionate students, who have a strong will to create new synthetic biology solutions. However, we could not do this without the support of our partners. We are especially grateful for the laboratory space and mentoring provided by Aalto University and its professors / other staff.

If you or your organization would like to support us in some way, contact us at team (at) aaltohelsinki.com. All of our supporters will be mentioned on this page and in our presentations and posters. Additional benefits, such as social media visibility can be negotiated.
