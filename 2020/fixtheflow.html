Fix the Flow

Fix the flow is a strategic and informative tower defence game aimed at younger people to educate them about wastewater treatment process and synthetic biology. The idea of the game is to acquire cleaning equipment and different types of bacteria in order to clean contamination from wastewater. As the game progresses, the difficulty level rises so the player -needs to upgrade the bacteria with new plasmids to keep up with the oncoming trash and pollution.
