Participate in the iGEM competition!

Do you want to spend an unforgettable summer?

Apply for (perhaps) the coolest summer job ever! We are bringing together a new iGEM team to represent Finland in an international competition.

Carry out your own ideas and experiments in a student-driven research group, design and build a biological system of your own and compete with it in Boston. Learn immense amounts of new things and spend an unforgettable summer with synthetic biology. Read more and apply!

iGEM?

  • international Genetically Engineered Machine
  • An international synthetic biology competition
  • A competition for university students pursuing either bachelor's or master's degree
  • Research in a student-driven group for a couple of months
  • Presenting your research and findings at the Giant Jamboree in Boston

Find out more about iGEM on http://igem.org/Main_Page.

You can take a look at the previous teams' websites on http://aaltohelsinki.com/2014/index_en.html, http://aaltohelsinki.com/2015 and http://aaltohelsinki.com/2016 . The teams' official wiki pages can be found on http://2014.igem.org/Team:Aalto-Helsinki, http://2015.igem.org/Team:Aalto-Helsinki and http://2016.igem.org/Team:Aalto-Helsinki.

This is what Aalto-Helsinki iGEM was in the previous years, but this year you and the rest of the team will decide what to research and how to do it!

What do you get?

  • Implement your own ideas with 6-12 other students in your own interdisciplinary research group.
  • Take part in the amazing competition in Boston with hundreds of teams from all around the world.
  • Learn new things that you never heard about in a lecture hall, in just one summer.
  • Find new unexpected career opportunities.
  • Work full-time over the summer - the ideation already starts in the spring, and work will continue in the fall.
  • An internationally substantial merit on your CV.
  • Take part in the search for funding and collaborating partners.
  • A trip to Boston and a paycheck, but don't expect significant monetary benefits.
  • An opportunity to get study credits.
  • Improve your English skills.
  • A chance to perfect your presentation skills and face new challenges.

We want you

    Information technology     Programming    Web-design     Statistics    
(Applied) Mathematics     Systems science     Communications    Medicine     Marketing    Design     Blogging    Photography     Presenting    Engineering     Product development    Mechatronics

Even if your field is not on the list, don't worry! Motivation and the desire to learn are the most important criteria!

Apply now!

The application period is open NOW and continues until January 29th 11.59 pm. You can apply by sending an email to ( rekry at aaltohelsinki . com ) according to the directions below. We will invite the most suitable candidates to an interview in the beginning of February and let you know about the decision by middle of February.

Write an open application letter where you consider at least the following questions:

  1. Why do you want to be a part of the 2017 iGEM team?
  2. What special skills of yours would be useful for the team?
  3. What would you like to learn during the summer?
  4. What kind of a team captain would you be? (only answer this question if you could see yourself in this position)

Attach the following documents to the email preferably as pdf-files:

  • Application letter
  • CV (please include your date of birth)
  • Transcript of completed university studies
